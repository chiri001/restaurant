About Us

At Ocean's Bounty, we are more than just a restaurant; we are a celebration of the ocean's treasures. With a passion for seafood and a deep love for the coastal lifestyle, we have crafted an extraordinary dining experience that combines the freshest catches from the sea with the warmth of genuine hospitality.

Our Story

Nestled on the shores of Boston, Ocean's Bounty was born from Alex Smith's lifelong connection to the sea. Our founder, the Alex Smith, was a fisherman and a seafood enthusiast. His dream was to create a place where the community could savor the finest seafood while feeling the ocean breeze and hearing the soothing waves. Today, his vision has become a reality, and we continue to share his passion with each guest who walks through our doors.
